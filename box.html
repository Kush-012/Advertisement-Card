Bhiwani

Bhiwani, bathed in the warm glow of June, offers a unique perspective on nature's symphony. The Aravalli Hills stand as silent sentinels, their rocky slopes dotted with hardy shrubs and acacia trees. The monsoon's approach paints the landscape with vibrant greens, the fields bursting with life as crops like bajra and cotton thrive. The air hums with the chirping of sparrows and the calls of koel birds. Peacocks strut proudly, their iridescent plumage shimmering in the sunlight. In the serene ponds, water lilies bloom, attracting a myriad of insects and small amphibians.
